## Executive Summary

GX402 is a comprehensive SDK and development framework that brings x402 payment protocol to the gaming industry across all major platforms. By providing ready-to-deploy integrations for Unity, Unreal Engine, Telegram Mini Games, Coinbase Mini Apps, Farcaster Frames, and mobile applications (iOS/Android), GX402 eliminates the complexity of implementing blockchain-based micropayments in games.
