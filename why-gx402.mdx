## Core Value Proposition

GX402 offers a unique value proposition designed to streamline game development and enhance player experiences:

*   **One SDK, All Platforms**: Seamlessly integrate across Unity, Unreal, Web, Mobile, and Mini Apps.
*   **Built-in x402 Facilitator Infrastructure**: Leverage a robust and efficient payment infrastructure.
*   **Multi-chain Support**: Compatible with both EVM and non-EVM blockchains.
*   **Fiat-to-Crypto Onramp Integration (Roadmap)**: Future-proof your game with easy fiat-to-crypto conversions.
*   **Genre-Specific Payment Templates for Gaming**: Utilize pre-built templates tailored for various game genres.
*   **Zero Blockchain Knowledge Required for Developers**: Focus on game development, not blockchain complexities.
